Why Bill Pay is Broken in Digital Banking

Most digital banking bill pay systems are built on outdated infrastructure and fail to meet modern customer expectations.

Customers expect real-time visibility, accurate due dates, and seamless payments. Instead, they get delays, uncertainty, and poor UX.

This creates friction, increases support calls, and reduces trust in the platform.

Fixing this requires better data integration, real-time processing, and a redesigned customer experience.
