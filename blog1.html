Why Bill Pay is Broken in Digital Banking

Bill pay is one of the most widely used features in digital banking—and one of the most broken.

Most systems rely on outdated infrastructure, resulting in delayed payments, unclear delivery timing, and poor customer visibility.

Customers expect real-time payments, accurate due dates, and seamless experiences. Instead, they get uncertainty—and that drives support calls, dissatisfaction, and churn risk.

Fixing this requires rethinking the entire stack: real-time rails, better data integration, and a redesigned UX built around transparency.
